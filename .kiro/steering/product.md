# Product Overview

This is a comprehensive Streamlit application for managing a dairy farm business. The app tracks expenses, milk sales, cow health records, and generates reports.

## Key Features

- **Dashboard**: Overview of daily stats and notifications for recurring expenses or cow events
- **Expenses**: Track farm expenses with support for recurring payments
- **Milk Sales**: Record daily milk sales per buyer and manage payments/advances with bulk daily production entry
- **Cows**: Manage cow records, vaccinations, yield history, and cow-specific expenses
- **Reports**: Generate and download CSV reports (Daily, Monthly, Buyer Ledgers, Cow Stats)
- **Dual Backend**: Runs completely offline (saving to JSON) OR syncs with Google Sheets

## Target Users

Dairy farm owners and managers who need to track their business operations, expenses, and milk production data either locally or with cloud synchronization.

## Business Logic

The app handles complex dairy farm operations including:
- Recurring expense tracking and notifications
- Buyer-specific milk sales with different rates
- Individual cow health and yield tracking
- Financial reporting and ledger management
- Payment and advance tracking for buyers
